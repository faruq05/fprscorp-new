Employee Retention Tax Credit

BUSINESS RECIEVE UP TO $26,000 PER EMPLOYEE

The Newest Federal Pandemic Program has been introduced via: The CARES Act (ERC) program - The Employee Retention Tax Credit gives companies up to $26,000 per each W2 Payroll employee on staff during the peak of the pandemic in 2020 & 2021 (receive up to 3.5mil max per business).


ABOUT ERC

The Employment Retention Credit (ERC) was introduced under the CARES Act to provide pandemic relief for businesses facing economic hardships. Our team of experts understands the nuances and eligibility requirements of the ERC, enabling us to help you claim the maximum credit for your business. By working with Fidelity, you can rest assured knowing that you're taking full advantage of this valuable tax credit.

The CARES Act offers several pandemic benefits aimed at supporting businesses during these challenging times. Our experienced professionals stay up-to-date with the latest changes to the CARES Act, ensuring you receive the most accurate guidance and advice. We'll help you identify and leverage every opportunity for financial relief under this legislation.


ERC QUALIFICATIONS

Companies that experienced partial shutdowns due to government orders limiting commerce, travel or group meetings; or that experienced significant declines in quarterly gross receipts (as compared to their quarterly gross receipts in 2019) due to the pandemic are eligible employers under this program.

* Must of Had 4 or more W2 Payroll Employees in 2020 and 2021 during the pandemic.
